Q Park, Deansgate

Q-Park Deansgate North is located underneath the new Embankment development. The car park is a short distance away from Manchester Arena, The Arndale Centre and Manchester Victoria Station.


Parking cannot be booked in advance, however you can access a 20% discount on your parking by validating your ticket with the hotel.

Directions from Q-Park to Motel One

It is a short walk to get to the hotel from the car park - take the left turn onto Victoria Bridge from Chapel St, before turning right onto Deansgate, left onto St Mary's Gate and finally a right onto Cross St to arrive the hotel. You can access the google maps by clicking on 'Directions from Q-Park to Motel One' above.
